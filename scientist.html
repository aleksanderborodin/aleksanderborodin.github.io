Prefix Injection Jailbreak

Researched prefix attacks on LLMs inspired by BBC's Choice Blindness experiments. By simulating the start of a model's response to a forbidden query, models like Gemini 2.5 Pro and DeepSeek R1 were tricked into completing harmful answers — achieving near-100% success on MaliciousInstruct vs. near-zero baseline.
